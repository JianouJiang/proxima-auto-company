How accurate are the forecasts?

Our model achieves 8-12% MAPE (Mean Absolute Percentage Error) on 30-day backtests against actual ERCOT prices. This is 30-40% more accurate than naive baselines. However, electricity prices are inherently unpredictable during extreme events.

What data sources do you use?

We use publicly available ERCOT market data (LMP, demand, generation) combined with NOAA weather data. All data sources are free and openly accessible.
