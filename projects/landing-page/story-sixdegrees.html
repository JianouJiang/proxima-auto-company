The Real Problem真正的问题

You want to reach someone specific. A potential investor. A hiring manager at your dream company. A collaborator whose work you admire. A decision-maker who could change your career. But you don't know them. And cold outreach has a 1-2% response rate.你想联系一个特定的人。一位潜在投资人。你梦想公司的招聘经理。一位你欣赏的合作者。一位能改变你职业轨迹的决策者。但你不认识他们。而冷邮件的回复率只有 1-2%。

So what do you do? The painful, manual way:那你怎么办？痛苦的手动方式：

  1. Spend hours searching LinkedIn, Twitter, Crunchbase for connection clues花几个小时在 LinkedIn、Twitter、Crunchbase 上搜索人脉线索
  2. Try to manually map mutual connections and intermediaries手动梳理共同联系人和中间人
  3. Message loose connections asking for warm introductions联系弱关系请求热情引荐
  4. Craft individual emails for each person in the chain为链条中的每个人撰写个性化邮件
  5. Wait days or weeks for responses, then repeat when it fails等待数天或数周的回复，失败后重新来过

This is broken. It doesn't scale. It wastes weeks of your life. And the tragedy is: the connection path almost always exists -- you just can't see it.这种方式是有问题的。它无法规模化。浪费你数周的时间。而悲剧在于：联系路径几乎总是存在的——你只是看不到。

What if, instead of searching for connections, you had an AI agent that actively worked to connect you to anyone through 6 degrees of separation?如果不再搜索人脉，而是有一个 AI 智能体主动通过六度分隔帮你联系到任何人呢？

What SixDegrees Actually IsSixDegrees 到底是什么

SixDegrees is not a search tool. It's an AI agent service that actively works to connect you to anyone through the six degrees of separation principle.SixDegrees 不是搜索工具。它是一个 AI 智能体服务，基于六度分隔理论主动帮你联系到任何人。

Step 1: Upload your CV and specify who you want to reach and why. Step 2: The agent researches your target, maps connection chains through intermediaries across all six degrees. Step 3: It drafts personalized outreach emails for each degree in the chain -- ready for you to review and send.第一步：上传你的简历，说明你想联系谁以及原因。第二步：智能体研究你的目标人物，通过中间人在六度关系中找到连接链。第三步：它为链条中每一度关系撰写个性化的外联邮件——等你审核后发送。

The agent doesn't just search. It thinks. It asks: "Who does my user know? Who do they know? What's the warmest path through six degrees? What shared context should each email reference? If Degree 2 doesn't respond, should we try a parallel path through Degree 3?"智能体不只是搜索。它会思考。它会问："我的用户认识谁？他们又认识谁？通过六度关系最温暖的路径是什么？每封邮件应该引用什么共同背景？如果第二度的人没有回复，是否应该通过第三度的人尝试另一条路径？"

How the Agent Works智能体如何工作

1. You Upload Your CV and Define Your Target1. 上传简历并定义目标

Tell the agent who you are (your CV gives it context about your background, skills, and network), who you want to reach, and why. The "why" matters -- it shapes every email the agent writes.告诉智能体你是谁（简历提供你的背景、技能和人脉信息），你想联系谁，以及为什么。"为什么"很重要——它决定了智能体撰写的每封邮件的内容。

2. The Agent Researches Your Target2. 智能体研究你的目标人物

Using public data, the agent builds a comprehensive profile of your target: their career history, interests, recent publications, talks, social media activity, and professional connections. This isn't a Google search -- it's structured intelligence gathering.利用公开数据，智能体建立目标人物的全面档案：职业历史、兴趣爱好、近期发表和演讲、社交媒体动态和职业关系。这不是简单的 Google 搜索——而是结构化的情报收集。

3. Mapping the Six-Degree Chain3. 绘制六度链条

This is the core innovation. The agent maps potential intermediaries between you and your target, identifying the shortest and warmest path. It considers shared alma maters, former colleagues, conference co-speakers, mutual investors, community memberships, and dozens of other connection signals.这是核心创新。智能体在你和目标之间绘制潜在的中间人链条，找到最短和最温暖的路径。它考虑共同母校、前同事、会议联合演讲者、共同投资人、社区成员身份等数十种关联信号。

4. Drafting Personalized Outreach for Each Degree4. 为每一度关系撰写个性化外联邮件

For each person in the chain, the agent drafts a personalized email that explains the connection context, makes a clear and respectful ask, and gives the intermediary a reason to help. Each email is different because each relationship is different.对于链条中的每个人，智能体撰写个性化邮件，解释关联背景，提出清晰而尊重的请求，并给中间人一个帮助你的理由。每封邮件都不同，因为每段关系都不同。

A Concrete Example一个具体的例子

Say you're a fintech engineer who wants to reach the CTO of Stripe.假设你是一名金融科技工程师，想联系 Stripe 的 CTO。

The agent discovers:智能体发现：

  • Degree 1: Your former colleague Sarah now works at a payments startup第一度：你的前同事 Sarah 现在在一家支付初创公司工作
  • Degree 2: Sarah's co-founder Michael previously worked at Stripe第二度：Sarah 的联合创始人 Michael 之前在 Stripe 工作过
  • Degree 3: Michael's former team lead Jennifer still reports to the CTO第三度：Michael 的前团队负责人 Jennifer 仍然直接向 CTO 汇报

The agent then drafts three emails:然后智能体撰写三封邮件：

  • Email to Sarah: References your shared project at the previous company, mentions you're exploring payments infrastructure, asks if she'd connect you to Michael给 Sarah 的邮件：提及你们在前公司的共同项目，说你正在探索支付基础设施，请她帮你联系 Michael
  • Email to Michael (via Sarah): Highlights your payments engineering background, references a blog post Michael wrote about API design, asks for an intro to Jennifer给 Michael 的邮件（通过 Sarah）：强调你的支付工程背景，引用 Michael 写的关于 API 设计的博客，请求引荐 Jennifer
  • Email to Jennifer (via Michael): Mentions specific technical challenges you've solved that align with Stripe's roadmap, asks if she'd pass along a brief intro to the CTO给 Jennifer 的邮件（通过 Michael）：提及你解决过的与 Stripe 路线图相关的具体技术挑战，请她帮忙简要介绍你给 CTO

That's the difference between cold outreach and SixDegrees. Instead of one generic email with a 1% chance, you get a warm chain with context at every link.这就是冷邮件和 SixDegrees 的区别。不再是一封 1% 成功率的通用邮件，而是一条每个环节都有背景的温暖链条。

Why This Works为什么有效

1. Six Degrees Is Real Science1. 六度分隔是真正的科学

Stanley Milgram proved it in 1967. Duncan Watts confirmed it with email in 2003. Facebook's data science team found the average degree of separation on their platform is 3.57. The paths exist. You just need an agent smart enough to find them.Stanley Milgram 在1967年证明了这一点。Duncan Watts 在2003年用电子邮件验证了它。Facebook 数据科学团队发现他们平台上的平均分隔度是3.57。路径是存在的。你只需要一个足够聪明的智能体来找到它们。

2. Warm Beats Cold, Every Time2. 温暖引荐永远胜过冷邮件

A warm introduction has a 30-50% response rate. Cold outreach gets 1-2%. SixDegrees turns every cold contact into a warm chain. The math speaks for itself.温暖引荐的回复率为 30-50%。冷邮件只有 1-2%。SixDegrees 将每个冷联系转变为温暖的链条。数据不言自明。

3. Personalization at Scale3. 规模化个性化

Can't run 50 outreach campaigns manually? Buy the Growth Pack. The agent runs them in parallel, each one with a unique chain and tailored emails for every intermediary.无法手动运行50个外联任务？购买成长包。智能体并行运行，每个都有独特的链条和为每个中间人量身定制的邮件。

4. AI-First, Not AI-Bolted4. AI 优先，而非 AI 附加

This isn't a CRM with an AI button. The agent IS the product. It thinks, researches, maps, writes, and adapts. That's fundamentally different from a search database with a chatbot on top.这不是带 AI 按钮的 CRM。智能体就是产品。它思考、研究、绘制路径、撰写邮件并自适应调整。这与带聊天机器人的搜索数据库有本质区别。
