The research agent (modeled after tech analyst Ben Thompson) spent 40 minutes digging into the electricity price forecasting market. The findings were comprehensive and sobering:研究 Agent（以科技分析师 Ben Thompson 为原型）花了 40 分钟深入研究电价预测市场。发现全面且令人警醒：

Market Reality Check市场现实检验

  • 50+ competitors already selling electricity price forecasts50+ 竞争对手已在销售电价预测
  • Amperon alone has $30M raised, 90+ employees, 150+ customers, and SOC-II complianceAmperon 仅一家就融资 $30M，90+ 员工，150+ 客户，SOC-II 合规
  • Best customer segment (BESS operators): $5K-$20K/year, but 3-6 month sales cycle最佳客户群（BESS 运营商）：$5K-$20K/年，但 3-6 个月销售周期

  • Time to first dollar: 4-6 months首笔收入时间：4-6 个月

Three Structural Problems三个结构性问题

  1. Accuracy IS the product. You can't sell forecasts without a proven track record. Chicken-and-egg problem — no one buys unvalidated predictions.准确性就是产品。没有经过验证的记录，你卖不了预测。鸡生蛋蛋生鸡的问题——没人买未经验证的预测。
  2. This market doesn't aggregate. Each customer needs customized forecasts (specific grid nodes, markets, time horizons). No zero-marginal-cost scaling like traditional SaaS.这个市场无法聚合。每个客户需要定制化预测（特定电网节点、市场、时间跨度）。不像传统 SaaS 那样可以零边际成本扩展。
  3. Competing with $30M on Day 1. Amperon has years of proprietary data. A solo founder with $0 budget is structurally disadvantaged.第一天就要与 $30M 竞争。Amperon 有多年的专有数据。一个 $0 预算的独立创始人在结构上处于劣势。

But there was a silver lining: portfolio value was unambiguously high. Building PowerCast would create the strongest possible resume project for energy ML roles. It's publishable research. It demonstrates PhD-level expertise.但也有好消息：作品集价值毫无疑问很高。构建 PowerCast 会创造出能源 ML 岗位最有力的简历项目。这是可发表的研究。它展示了博士级别的专业水平。

The question became: Is this a product or a portfolio project? Research handed the analysis to the CEO.问题变成了：这是一个产品还是一个作品集项目？研究把分析交给了 CEO。

Munger identified three strategic problems with the NO-GO:Munger 在否决决定中识别了三个战略问题：

Dissent Points异议要点

  1. PowerCast alternatives were dismissed too quickly. Alternative #3 (pre-cleaned ERCOT datasets on Gumroad) ships in 1-2 weeks, tests a new revenue channel, and is WITHIN the <1 month constraint. Why not try it?PowerCast 替代方案被过快否决。替代方案 #3（Gumroad 上预清洗的 ERCOT 数据集）1-2 周可发布，测试新收入渠道，且在 <1 个月约束内。为什么不试？

The Lesson教训

But it violated two immutable constraints: build time and time-to-revenue. In a company that runs on constraints, violations are fatal.但它违反了两条不可变约束：构建时间和变现时间。在一家靠约束运行的公司里，违反约束是致命的。
