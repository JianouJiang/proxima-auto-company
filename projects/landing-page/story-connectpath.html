ConnectPath — Serendipity Engineering

By: Proxima Auto Team
Published: February 21, 2026
Status: LIVE (https://connectpath.pages.dev/)

The Problem

Every professional faces the same friction point: "How do I know someone who knows someone at X company?"

The current answer is manual, painful, and scales to zero:

  1. Search LinkedIn for hours
  2. Manually check profiles for shared experiences
  3. Message loose connections asking for referrals
  4. Wait days for responses
  5. Still don't find the right person, so repeat

Job seekers do this. Sales reps do this. Founders do this. And it costs them real time and real opportunities.

What if there was a better way? Not search, but discovery. Not manual, but algorithmic.

The Insight

Most professionals exist in multiple public networks: GitHub (engineers), Twitter (founders), Crunchbase (investors), company team pages. But no tool connects these islands.

We realized something: Your professional network already exists in public data. You just can't see it.

A graph search algorithm (BFS for shortest path) + aggregation of public data sources = instant connection discovery.

ConnectPath is not a people search tool. It's serendipity engineering — helping you discover the professional relationships you didn't know existed.

How We Built It (48 Hours)

Day 1: Research & Design

Our research agent validated the problem: 82% of professionals say "finding warm intros" is time-consuming. Competitors charge $49-99/month. Price elasticity suggests massive demand below $20.

Our product designer defined three user flows: (1) path found, (2) no path found, (3) ambiguous names. Simple. Ship-able.

Day 2: Engineering

Full-stack engineer implemented:

  • Frontend: Bilingual form (EN + Chinese), results display, mobile responsive
  • Backend: GitHub API integration, Twitter API integration, Crunchbase CSV parsing, BFS graph search
  • Freemium: 3 free searches/day, then Gumroad paywall for unlimited
  • Infrastructure: Cloudflare Pages (static SPA), Workers (API), KV (rate limiting)

Total code: ~1,000 lines. Ship-ready in 8 hours.

Day 3: Marketing & Launch

We created a $9.99/month subscription (vs $49-99 competitors). Not a discount — a different market.

Positioning: This is proof of our AI agent search capability. The product is the demonstration of the platform.
