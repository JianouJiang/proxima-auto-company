ConnectPath — Your Personal AI Agent That Reaches Anyone

By: Proxima Auto Team
Published: February 21, 2026
Status: LIVE V2 (https://baf83e1e.connectpath.pages.dev/)

The Real Problem

You want to reach someone specific. A potential investor. A hiring manager. A collaborator. But you don't know them directly.

So what do you do? The painful, manual way:

  1. Spend hours searching LinkedIn, Twitter, Crunchbase
  2. Try to manually map mutual connections
  3. Message loose connections asking for warm intros
  4. Wait days (or weeks) for responses
  5. Often fail anyway, so repeat the whole process

This is broken. It doesn't scale. It wastes weeks of your life.

What if, instead of searching for connections, you had an AI agent that actually worked to reach people on your behalf?

What ConnectPath Actually Is

ConnectPath is not a search tool. It's an AI agent service that researches your target person, maps 6-degree connection chains, and drafts personalized outreach emails — all automatically.

You: Upload your CV, specify who you want to reach, explain why. Agent: Goes to work researching, mapping, drafting. Result: Personalized outreach strategy ready to execute.

The agent doesn't just search. It thinks. It asks: "Who does my user know? Who do they know? What's the warmest path? What should we say in the first email? Should we try a different intermediary if this one doesn't respond?"

The Story: Two Versions

Cycle 61: We Built Wrong

In our first attempt, we built a simple GitHub graph search tool — enter two names, it finds the shortest path between them using public GitHub data.

It worked. It was fast. It was shipped in 48 hours.

But it wasn't what the founder wanted. It was passive. It was just search.

Cycle 62: We Rebuilt Right

The founder said: "This isn't a search tool. It's an AI agent service. The agent should actively work to reach people on the user's behalf."

So we rebuilt from scratch. In 45 minutes, we shipped the real ConnectPath — an AI agent service powered by Claude.

Here's the architecture:

  • Frontend: Intake form (upload CV, specify target, explain motivation) + dashboard (track campaigns, credit usage)
  • Backend: Cloudflare Workers + D1 database + Claude API
  • AI Agent: 3-step pipeline per campaign:
    • Step 1: Research the target person (1 credit)
    • Step 2: Find potential intermediaries (1 credit)
    • Step 3: Draft personalized outreach emails (1 credit)
  • Pricing: Credit-based (agent work = credits consumed)

Why This Matters

V1 was a proof-of-concept. V2 is a real business.

V1 could tell you: "Path found! Sarah → Michael → Jennifer → your target."

V2 tells you: "Your target is CEO of Stripe. They studied at Stanford and worked at Goldman. Likely interested in payments and risk management. Sarah knows someone who interned with them. Here's a draft email emphasizing your payments expertise. Want to approve and send?"

That's the difference between a tool and an agent.
