Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

1. Who We Are

Proxima Auto ("we", "us", "our") operates software products including ColdCopy and Double Mood. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. What We Collect

  • ColdCopy: Job title, company name, and email content you provide to generate cold email sequences. Payment information is processed by Stripe (we never see your full card number).
  • Double Mood: Mood selections and breathing exercise data stored locally on your device (localStorage). We do not collect or transmit this data to any server.
  • Analytics: We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which collects anonymous page view data (no cookies, no personal identifiers).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the service you requested (generate emails, track mood)
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To understand aggregate usage patterns (anonymous analytics only)

4. Data Storage

  • ColdCopy: Session data stored in Cloudflare D1 (encrypted at rest). Data is retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted.
  • Double Mood: All data stays on your device in localStorage. Nothing is sent to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — Payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudflare — Hosting and analytics (Cloudflare Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI text generation for ColdCopy (Anthropic Privacy Policy)

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request deletion of your ColdCopy data by emailing us
  • Clear your Double Mood data anytime by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • Opt out of analytics by using a browser with tracking protection

7. Cookies

We do not use cookies. Cloudflare Web Analytics is cookie-free.

8. Children

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
